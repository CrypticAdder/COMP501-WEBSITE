Databreaches / Hackers

Security for our online presence can be as important as our safety in real life, as it can be much easier to get your information stolen and used for unethical practices. Threats of cyberattacks are a big problem now a days especially when our worlds relies on online heavily too. Databreaches can be linked to hackers and just any type of breach on private information and doxxing. Hackers and data breaches are a big concern because it can affect businesses and even just normal individuals. Reasons we chose this topic was because it is an ever-growing problem, and we need to realise the risks and severity of hackers breaching certain networks. As mentioned before, some key important topics that can be delved into are data breaches/privacy concerns/hackers.

Data breaches has a vast array of ethical issues, it can negatively affect individuals, organizations, businesses. Quality of life can be affected if sums of money was stolen by a data breach or hackers. As mentioned before privacy can be at risked, which could lead to personal photos being leaked, private information or important documents can be leaked. Deception is a key factor to hackers' success where they can pretend to be a trusted organisation to steal information from unsuspected users such as elderly people or kids.
