Technology Opportunities

Although databreaches and hackers/hacking have mostly negative outcomes, opportunities are still avaialable in this topic. Preventing data leaks or breaches is having a system that can prevent it or detect the breach. This is called Data leak prevnetion and detection systems (DLPD). They help obvsiouly to prevent and identify breaches. Systems such as firewall or antivirus softwares are used for basic protection but DPLD specifically deals data leakage threats. These systems like these requires developers and other type of software workers to develop such systems, giving job opportunities to people who can prevent these cyber attacks against users and businesses. Overtime as more people work in these fields, innovations will be made for our online security which can benefit national security, technological advancements and better and more reliable privacy and data protection services.
